
# Audits and Assessments


## Attestation 
 : Verifying and validating the accuracy, realiability, and effectiveness of security controls, systems, and processes implemented within an organization

## Internal Assessment
 : An audit report that focuses on the organizations needs to support its current infrastructure

## External Assessment
 : An audit report that enures the organizations practices are measured against recognized industry standards
