## Background Checks
 : A process which determines if a persone is who they say they are and are not concealing criminal activity, bankruptcy, or connections that would make them unsuitable or risky

## Onboarding
 : The process of bringing in a new employee, contractor, or supplier

## Offboarding
 : The process of ensuring that all HR and other requirements are covered when an employee leaves and organization

## Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
 : A policy that governs employees use of company equipment and internet services

## Code of Conduct

 : A organizations policy that mandates employees work areas be free from potentially sensitive information
