
# Risk Management


## Risk Management
 : The process of identifying potential issues, assessing their potential impact on the organization, and implementing controls to mitigate them
 * Identifying mission essential functions, vulnerabilities, and threats
 * Analyze how it impacts the bussiness and identify risk response

## Business Impact Analysis(BIA)
 : A process that helps businesses identify organizational risks and determines their effect on ongoing, mission critical operations
 * Main goal is to proactively create recovery strategirs to minimize the impace of any disruptions

## Missions Essential Functions(MEF)
 : A business activity that is too critical to be deferred for anything more than a few hours, if at all
